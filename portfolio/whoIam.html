Highly ambitious and extremely talented graphic designer bringing with me over ten years of Italian International experience as well as continued education to my role. Art and design literally flows in my blood, since my grandpa in Milan was a graphic designer around the ‘60.

My Grandfather at work in the 60,s.
He was one of the first Graphic Designer in Milan, he made a lot of ADV campaingns for big companies like: Olivetti.

With that I hope to bring a fresh and unique perspective to the role, whether working alone or as part of a larger team. I strive to master the art of all mediums of communications and advertising. I am passionate and driven about creating and designing. I love the challenges that each new project brings. For me, every shape, every font and every color, must be thoroughly explored in order to find the client their winning combination, and ultimately deliver to them a brand product or logo that exceeds their expectations.
